Recommender Page

Recommender page is currently down due to API changes.

How it works: I trained and applied the collaborative machine learning model to a large dataset of my own creation - some of it from UCSD's Goodread's Database, most of it webscraped from reviews off of Amazon, Barnes&Noble and other places. To use it, simply enter a genre, character, author or artist, and receive recommendations for their work from the algorithm!
